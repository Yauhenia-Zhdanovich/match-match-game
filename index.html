Match-Match Game

  • Difficulty :

Match-Match Game is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn.

How to play

  • the goal of the game - find all the pairs ( our cards should disappear )
  • pick two cards you like the most and click on them
  • if the pictures on the faces of the cards are the same,we've done with this two and are able to move forward to remaining ones. Otherwise, keep clicking
  • top tip: don't forget to injoy the game!

To start a new game click the 'New Game' button
